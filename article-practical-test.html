It's time for Practical Test!

Today we're having a practical test. Refer to this guide on how to do it.

Scoring criteria

Criteria Description Max score
Creating special slides You should be able to create other forms of slides, such as header section or two contents. 30
List bullet hierarchy You should be able to shift the bullets forwards and backwards using the tab or shift-tab key. 15
Pictures You should be able to add pictures from the internet without crashing the PowerPoint. 20
Presentation writing skill You should be able to write your presentation well (keep it short, make it a list, make bold important words). 30
Total max score 95

Notes

  • You may use the internet, though remember, rewrite it nicely; don't just copy-paste it.
  • The text doesn't necessarily be similar to mine. Just make it a good presentation writing.
  • As the example of vehicles, put three of them. In each, put at least one picture.

Format of the slides

Slide 1 - The Title

Layout: Title Slide. Of course, the subtitle will be your own name and grade.

Slide 2 - What is Transportation

Layout: Title and Content.

Slide 3 - Kinds of Vehicle

Layout: Two Content. Don't forget to shift the list bullet inwards.

Slide 4 - Other Forms of Transportation

Layout: Title and Content. You actually don't need to put images here. I'm just putting this for fun.

Slide 5 - Section: Examples of Vehicle

Layout: Section Header.

Slide 6, 7, 8 (or more) - Each Example of Vehicles

Layout: Title and Content. The easiest thing to put in here is (though you don't necessarily follow this):

  • What kind of transportation it is,
  • What does it look like or any other physical attributes,
  • What is it used for.

Final Slide - Closing

Layout: Title Slide.
